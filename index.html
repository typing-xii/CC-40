About Us

Divya Yatra is your spiritual companion to sacred pilgrimages across India. We guide you through places of deep spiritual energy and divine significance.

Featured Destinations

Kedarnath

Located in the Himalayas, Kedarnath is one of the holiest shrines dedicated to Lord Shiva. Surrounded by snow-clad peaks, it is a center of spiritual awakening.

Badrinath

Dedicated to Lord Vishnu, Badrinath is one of the Char Dhams. Nestled between the Nar and Narayan mountains, it’s a divine destination of devotion and beauty.

Gangotri

The origin of the sacred Ganges River. A serene and high-altitude pilgrimage site that is ideal for inner peace and connection to nature’s divinity.
